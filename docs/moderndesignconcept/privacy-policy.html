Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Overview

Modern Design Concept ("we", "our") operates a Pinterest integration app used solely for publishing our own product designs to Pinterest. This app is for internal use only and does not collect, store, or share personal data from third parties.

Data Collection

This application accesses the Pinterest API exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with our own Pinterest Business account
  • Create and manage pins on our own boards
  • Create and manage boards on our own account

No data from other Pinterest users is collected, stored, or processed.

Data Storage

OAuth access tokens are stored locally on our own devices and are not transmitted to any third-party servers. Tokens are used solely to authenticate API requests to Pinterest.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties.
